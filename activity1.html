SDG 4: Quality Education

Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.

Why It Matters

Education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty. It empowers people with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to shape a better future.

Targets of SDG 4

  • Free primary and secondary education for all
  • Equal access to affordable technical and higher education
  • Increase the number of qualified teachers
  • Eliminate gender disparities in education

What You Can Do

Support organizations that provide learning materials, volunteer to teach, or donate to educational causes. Every action counts!
